How I work · 03

A process built on
research, not guesswork.

01

Discover & research

Deep-dive into users, competitors and business goals. Interviews, surveys, and analytics to understand what matters.

02

Define & ideate

Translate insights into user flows, information architecture and opportunity maps. Narrow the fog into a clear brief.

03

Design & iterate

Wireframes, hi-fi mockups, design system tokens. Build in public with the team. Iterate against user feedback.

04

Ship & measure

Dev handoff with detailed specs. Ship, measure against KPIs, iterate. The work isn't done at launch — it starts there.
